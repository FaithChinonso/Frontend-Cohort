Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish from Rome.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs and cheese together.
  4. Toss everything off the heat. Serve immediately.
